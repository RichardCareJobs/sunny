Privacy

Sunny uses your browser’s geolocation (with your permission) to show venues near you. Your location does not leave your device.

When fetching venues we query public OpenStreetMap data via Overpass API. Requests are made directly from your browser to Overpass.

No personal information is collected, stored, or shared by Sunny. If you install the app, your device may cache files for offline use.

Questions? Contact: hello@sunny.example (update to your real email).
